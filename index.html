The Studio

Wemaiq is a boutique creative studio based in Canada.

Founded by Brigitte K and Alex M, the studio brings together decades of experience across user experience, product design, and service design. Together they have led organizations, shaped product strategy, and built digital systems used by millions of people.

Today their work focuses on designing and building thoughtful software — tools grounded in human needs, clear thinking, and careful craft.

How We Work

We believe the best tools emerge from a deep understanding of people.

Our approach combines product thinking, design craft, and systems thinking to create software that is simple, durable, and genuinely useful. We focus on clarity, usability, and long-term product thinking rather than trends.

About

Function
+ Feeling.

We believe things should work. That the care put into making something is felt by the people who use it — a connection from creator to creation.

So on every project we start with two questions: what does it do, and how does using it make people feel? Our approach combines product thinking, design craft, and systems thinking to create software that is simple, durable, and genuinely useful. We focus on clarity, usability, and long-term product thinking rather than trends.
